# AFK

**Description:**

Adds AFK to your nickname and shows why are you AFK

#### Usage:

```text
W!afk [reason]
```

#### Permissions:

```text
No special permissions needed for this command
```

#### Arguments:

```text
[reason] - reason why are you AFK
```

#### Syntax:

```text
{} - Required.
[] - Optional.
```
